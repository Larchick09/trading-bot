# 🚀 HOW TO DEPLOY YOUR TRADING BOT TO RAILWAY
# ================================================
# Follow these steps ONE TIME and the bot runs forever

## STEP 1 — Upload bot files to GitHub (free)
1. Go to github.com and create a free account
2. Click "New repository"
3. Name it: trading-bot
4. Upload these files:
   - bot.py
   - requirements.txt
   - railway.toml

## STEP 2 — Connect Railway to GitHub
1. Go to railway.app (you already have an account)
2. Click "New Project"
3. Click "Deploy from GitHub repo"
4. Select your trading-bot repository
5. Railway will automatically detect it's Python

## STEP 3 — Add your secret keys (Variables)
In Railway, click your project → "Variables" tab
Add each of these:

  TRADOVATE_USER     → your Tradovate username
  TRADOVATE_PASS     → your Tradovate password
  CLAUDE_API_KEY     → get from console.anthropic.com
  GOOGLE_DRIVE_BRAIN_FOLDER_ID → from Google Drive URL

## STEP 4 — Deploy!
Click "Deploy" — Railway builds and starts your bot.
You'll see live logs showing the bot starting up.

## STEP 5 — Phone alerts (optional)
1. Sign up at pushover.net ($5 one-time)
2. Add PUSHOVER_TOKEN and PUSHOVER_USER to Railway variables
3. You'll get phone notifications for every trade

## THAT'S IT!
The bot now runs 24/7 on Railway's servers.
It wakes up every trading day at 6 AM CT automatically.
You never need to touch your computer for it to run.

## HOW TO UPDATE THE BOT LATER
Just tell Claude what you want changed.
Claude updates bot.py.
You upload the new file to GitHub.
Railway automatically redeploys. Done.
